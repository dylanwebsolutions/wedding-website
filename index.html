Getting Here

Sydney, Australia

We're so excited to celebrate in one of the world's most beautiful cities. Here's everything you need to make your visit seamless.

✈

Getting Here

Fly into Sydney Kingsford Smith Airport (SYD). From there, head north on the Northern Beaches Busway or grab a rideshare — Palm Beach is about an hour from the airport depending on traffic.

🏡

Where to Stay

We recommend staying in the Northern Beaches — close to both venues and one of Sydney's most beautiful stretches of coastline. We'll be based in a large Airbnb home in Palm Beach for the weekend and can't wait to have everyone nearby.

🌊

Explore the Beaches

Make a weekend of it — swim at Whale Beach, walk the Barrenjoey Lighthouse track, or catch a ferry from Palm Beach to Ettalong. November weather is warm, sunny, and perfect for it.
